Pastor Kalu Okorie

The visionary behind Karmo Development Center, Pastor Kalu Okorie, was called by God to rescue children from the streets. His remarkable journey from aeronautics engineering to missionary work spans decades of dedicated service.

As a former radar technology specialist, Pastor Kalu brings unique insights to child rescue operations, combining technical precision with compassionate ministry.
