The Vision Bearer

Pastor Kalu Okorie

From Aeronautics Engineering to Divine Mission: A Journey of Faith, Sacrifice, and Transforming Lives

"For I know the plans I have for you," declares the Lord, "plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future." - Jeremiah 29:11

An Extraordinary Journey

The remarkable story of Pastor Kalu Okorie's transformation from a successful engineer to a dedicated missionary, answering God's call to rescue children from the streets.

Early Years

The Aeronautics Engineer

Before answering God's call, Pastor Kalu Okorie was an accomplished aeronautics engineer specializing in radar technology. His technical expertise and analytical mind positioned him for a successful career in aviation technology, working on cutting-edge systems that would shape modern air travel.

The Divine Call

From Engineering to Ministry

In a life-changing moment, God called Pastor Kalu into ministry. Leaving behind his promising engineering career, he embraced his new mission with the same dedication and precision he applied to radar technology—now focused on scanning hearts instead of skies.

Missionary Work

Decades of Service

For over three decades, Pastor Kalu has served as a dedicated missionary, bringing hope and transformation to communities across nations. His missionary work spans continents, touching lives through evangelism, community development, and humanitarian outreach.

The Vision

Rescuing Street Children

In a powerful vision from God, Pastor Kalu received a specific mission: to take children off the streets. This divine mandate became the foundation for what would become the Karmo Development Center—a beacon of hope for vulnerable children.

Karmo Development Center

Birth of a Legacy

Combining his engineering precision with divine compassion, Pastor Kalu established the Karmo Development Center. The center represents the perfect fusion of technical excellence and spiritual purpose, creating sustainable solutions for street children.

The Mission & Vision

Pastor Kalu's unique background in engineering and ministry creates a distinctive approach to humanitarian work, combining systematic thinking with compassionate action.

The Divine Mandate

To rescue children from the streets and provide them with education, life skills, and opportunities for a brighter future, guided by Christian principles and values.

Engineering Mindset

Applying systematic problem-solving and innovative thinking from aeronautics engineering to create sustainable solutions for social challenges.

Missionary Heart

Decades of missionary experience inform a compassionate, holistic approach to child development that addresses spiritual, emotional, and physical needs.

"God showed me that just as radar technology detects objects invisible to the naked eye, we must develop spiritual radar to see the potential in every street child—potential that the world has overlooked."

— Pastor Kalu Okorie

The Uniqueness of His Approach

Pastor Kalu's background as an aeronautics engineer specializing in radar technology gives him a unique perspective on his mission. He often says:

"Just as radar systems detect and track objects through all conditions, we must develop systems to detect, track, and nurture the potential in every child, no matter how hidden or obscured by life's circumstances."

This engineering mindset, combined with decades of missionary experience, creates a powerful approach to humanitarian work that is both systematic and deeply compassionate.
